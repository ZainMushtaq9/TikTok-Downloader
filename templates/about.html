About Xainvex

Xainvex was created to provide a simpler, faster, and smarter way to download media from the internet. The internet changes quickly, and so do the ways videos are stored. We've built an AI-driven backend that constantly adapts to provide reliable downloads across all major platforms.

Why use Xainvex?

  • AI Powered: We don't just extract; our AI automatically recommends the best format and summarizes videos before you download.
  • Free Forever: No premium tiers, no hidden fees, and no frustrating pop-up labyrinths. We rely on standard display ads to keep the servers running.
  • Privacy First: We do not ask for registration or maintain a database of the files you transfer. What you download stays between you and the host server.
  • Multi-Platform: We support YouTube, TikTok, Reddit, Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Vimeo, Twitch, and more.
