DMCA Policy

Xainvex respects the intellectual property rights of others. We comply with the provisions of the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

1. No Hosted Content

It is important to understand that we do not host any files on our servers. Xainvex acts merely as a conduit, fetching and passing a temporary stream from the public host (e.g., YouTube) directly to your browser. We have no control over the content hosted on third-party domains.

2. Takedown Requests

However, if you are a copyright owner and would like us to block our service from processing URLs pointing to your specific content, we will accommodate your request. Please send a formal DMCA Notice to our designated agent including:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • The exact URL(s) on the originating platform (e.g. YouTube video URL) that you want us to block.
  • Information reasonably sufficient to permit us to contact you, such as an address, telephone number, and email.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner.

Email your notice to: dmca@Xainvex.example.com

We aim to process all valid requests within 72 hours.
