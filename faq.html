Troubleshooting

Why is my download not starting?

If downloads aren't starting, try these solutions:

  • Check if the video URL is correct and publicly accessible

  • Try a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions that might block downloads
  • Check your internet connection

Why does it say "Failed to download"?

Common reasons include:
