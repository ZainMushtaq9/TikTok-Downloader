Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user registration. The only data we collect automatically is standard web analytics via Google Analytics, such as IP address (anonymised), browser type, and timestamps for performance monitoring and AdSense compliance.

2. Video Downloads

We do not store any videos downloaded through our service. All media is streamed directly from the host platform (YouTube, TikTok, etc.) to your device. We do not keep logs of which specific URLs you download.

3. Cookies

We use local storage strictly for functional purposes (e.g. saving your theme preference and local download history). Third-party vendors like Google use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings.

4. Third Party Links

Our AI extraction tool generates summaries and metadata from urls. We send only the text metadata to Anthropic/Google AI services; we do not send any identifiable user data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please email: privacy@Xainvex.example.com
